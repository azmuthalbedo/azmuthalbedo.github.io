Meet Chris

Chris is a writer and novice programmer, but first a thinker. He spends most of his time researching, analyzing and articulating thoughts in concise formats while preserving cognitive precision. He sees himself as a noble Knight wielding the sword of knowledge against ignorance.

Cyberspace is also one of the tools he employs for his quest. But here, he's a Wizard and not a Knight, given his ability to manipulate cyberspace wielding the magic of codes.

He lives by the following principles;
"Logic first in all things"
"The knowledge of a thing is the destruction of its mystery, therefore seek knowledge".

He's currently studying computer science at the of Federal University of Technology, Minna.
